Alistair Vane, a 50 year old rich man has been murdered! It is you job to figure out who. This is written in python.

## Features
In this game, you can do 3 things:

1. Look at the victim - Look at the autopsy report, look at medical records, read their story

2. Investigate Rooms - The kitchen, the bedroom, the bathroom, and the living room. Each room has multiple items to inspect

3. Interrogate the suspects - You can interrogate the cook, the butler, the daughter, and a former business partner. You can choose which questions to ask, and if you find certain evidence during your investigation, you can unlock questions to ask?
